## Typography & Design Best Practices

- **Contrast:** Ensure text placed on top of these colors meets WCAG accessibility guidelines.
- **Accents:** Use the secondary colors to draw attention to call-to-actions, data points in dashboards, and interactive hover states.
- **Backgrounds:** Pair these colors with light or dark neutral backgrounds to allow the brand colors to stand out effectively.
